How to choose correct model:

1. AR: look at autocorrelations. pick AR term according to PACF. ideally, when ACF tapers, AR(1) is ideal. another "ideal" model shows sinusiodal ACF and PACF. This indicates the use of AR(2).
2. MA: for an MA(1) model ideally autocorrelation should show some sinusiodal structure with non-significant spikes at higher lags
3. combined ARMA model: if there is a lot of memory in the system, where current values are determined both by previous values, as well as random-noise and random noise patterns influenced by previous random noise patterns.
-> data seems to indicate an ARMA model
