Story Point 4: Modelling Results (2 Min)

**What to show:**
- Actual prices (black line, fixed)
- Different models. 
	- Persistence (same as yesterday: RMSE = 30.76) / Seasonal naive (same as last year RMSE = 56.42): Awful
	- ARIMA only: Full period (2015-2023): ARIMA(2,1,0), RMSE = 28.40 - not very good, thinks its back in calm periods, cannot deal with increased volatility and cannot track trend
	- ARIMA only: Shorter period (2020-2023): ARIMA(3,0,0), RMSE = 26.23  - better, actually tracks a slightly positive trend but still doesn´t adapt to volatility
	Interpretation: Structural breaks destroyed autocorrelation patterns	

	We need external variables:
	- ARIMAX (3,0,0) with gas
	- Gas only: RMSE = 21.14 is best model
	- Tried Gas + CO2 = 25.64 (CO2 non-significant, p=0.58)
	- Other models even worse
	- Complete model not possible due to multicollinearity

	-> Gas prices provide stable external signal → Better generalization
		- AR/MA terms needed when external variable is this strong (Gas explains 83% of standardized variance), no variance left to be explained by AR/MA terms, no memory in time series left
		- Simpler model, better interpretable
